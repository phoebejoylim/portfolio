Phoebe Joy Lim

Ethos

Stemming from my interest in poetry and painting as a kid, my philosophy for design has always influenced by my love of art. I love thinking about the many possibilities of a project when I start, painting in broad strokes, then refining gradually.

I firmly believe that to design means to understand people’s needs and behaviours, and my goal in life is to do work for the greater good. As a woman of colour in a male-dominated field, I feel that diversity and intersectionality are essential to moving design forward, and try to incorporate it in the projects I do.

Interests

When I’m not designing, I love to binge watch animated series on Netflix, make (and eat) artisanal popsicles, and play with my dog Oreo.
